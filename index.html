Rascal Flatts

Fan Page by Nick Kelly

Who they are

Rascal Flatts is an American country pop music band, and they were formed in 1999 in Nashville, Tennessee. Band members include Gary LeVox, Jay DeMarcus, and Joe Don Rooney. LeVox and DeMarcus are second cousins. Both LeVox and DeMarcus originate from Columbus, Ohio.

Career

Their debut album, self-titled Rascal Flatts, was released in 2000. Their first number 1 single called "These Days" came from their second album Melt, which was released in 2002. Between 2000 and 2010 they recorded for Disney Music Group's Lyric Street Records division. During their time with that lable, they released six studio albums, which were all certified platinum or higher.

Accomplishments

Rascal Flatts released more than forty singles, sixteen of them reached number 1 on Billboard Hot Country Songs. The bands longest running number 1, "Bless the Broken Road", spent five weeks atop Hot Country Songs in 2005. They also found great success with "Life Is a Highway", which was recorded for the soundtrack of the Dinsey/Pixar film, Cars. The group was named vocal group of the year by the CMA every year between 2003 and 2008, top vocal group by the AMA every year between 2003 and 2009, and they also won the title artist of the year by AMA in 2006.
